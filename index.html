Who we are

Nari Sewa Kendra identifies and provides for the needs of poor and disadvantaged women.

We manage a wide range of activities and services to enable the needy, in particular women and the underprivileged for their livelihood, self-reliance, and confidence in decision making.

Target Groups

  • Women
  • People with limited access to education, food, shelter
  • Minority Groups including dalits

Activities

  • Gender Equity program
  • Health and Sanitation
  • Women Empowerment
  • Participatory Approach through Holistic Development (Path)
  • Advocacy/Campaign/Lobbying
  • Skills Training (e.g. sewing and weaving)

Impact

  • Income generation through microlending
  • Peace building assistance for war-torn communities
  • Straw weaving training
  • Human rights advocacy
  • Women leadership development training
  • Awareness on women/girls trafficking
