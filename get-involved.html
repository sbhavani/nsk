Get Involved

Nari Sewa Kendra (NSK) is an exemplary and uniquely run (not for profit) NGO by a group of local women for over three decades now. It is kept alive by the persistent efforts of women. We provide many opportunities for the people of Western Nepal including funds for education, money for training, provision of meals, programs targeting health and seed money for individuals to initiate their own small income generating businesses. Here are a few ways you can get involved with Nari Sewa Kendra:

Sponsor an orphanage

You may wish denote funds that will be used to help orphaned children in Kaski district, Nepal. Through your donation to NSK, these orphans will be provided proper education, food and medical care. In the longer term, your donation will mean that these children will in time be able to earn their own living and become empowered in their own lives, assisting others such as themselves while fully participating in Nepal’s future.

Sponsor a poor woman

Through your donation, you may sponsor a woman so that she will receive training, resource materials and seed money to initiate her own entrepreneurship. Your donation will not only directly assist this woman but will also reach her entire family, benefiting all whom live within this family and touching the entire community. Five hundred US dollars will not only change a women’s life forever, but will also cover all costs for her familfamily's care, creating opportunities for all involved.

Donate goods

NSK is always open for donations, of any size, of school and home goods, including kitchenware. No donation is too large or too small – every item can change a life for the better. We welcome new and gently used supplies of the following types:

  • Books
  • School stationery
  • Sports equipment
  • Computers & computer equipment
  • School bags
  • Clothes
  • Shoes & socks
  • Kitchen utensils

Volunteer/Internship

Volunteering is a great way to be a part of both NSK’s long-term and day-to-day initiatives. You can be a part of the change and empowerment that is at the heart of all the NSK does within the communities. NSK invites people from around the world to share their skills, knowledge and time in making a difference to women and the poor living in Nepal.

In order to qualify as a volunteer for NSK one must:

  • Have no major health problems
  • Be eligible to obtain a tourist visa to Nepal
  • Flexible, committed and versatile

Please contact NSK for further information and plan for an internship/volunteer position.

Partner with us

Help spread the word regarding NSK and its initiatives! We’re always happy to speak with anyone who may be interested in our programs. You are welcome to Visit us at any time of the year as we are stationed in the beautiful valley of Pokhara, in Nepal. You are invited to stop by and see all that NSK is accomplishing. Your observation and feedback will be highly appreciated. We always enjoy meeting new people and look forward to saying Namaste! NSK can also arrange a cost-effective, social trek around the valley and mountain area. Please inquire further if interested.
